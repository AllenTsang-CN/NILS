NILS THE NEW ADVENTURE

Rediscover the Magic of a Classic Tale!
Based on Selma Lagerlöf’s beloved 1906 novel, Nils Holgersson’s Wonderful Adventures, this modern adaptation brings a timeless story to life for today’s audiences. Join Nils, a mischievous boy turned hero, as he embarks on an extraordinary journey across Sweden, learning the true meaning of friendship, courage, and responsibility.

Why You’ll Love It:
1. Classic IP with Global Appeal: Nils Holgersson is a cherished character in European literature, offering a strong foundation for international success.
2. Hollywood Director Jason Reisig: Known for his work on How to Train Your Dragon and Kung Fu Panda, Reisig brings his visionary storytelling to this enchanting tale.
3. Modern Narrative for Today’s Kids: This reimagined story explores themes of empathy, self-discovery, and environmental awareness, resonating with contemporary audiences.
